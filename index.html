This project focused on democratizing access to cloud computing by enabling users‚Äîespecially those without technical expertise‚Äîto provision and manage cloud resources using natural language through an LLM-powered agent.

Web & Mobile + Offline Mode

AI for Smallholder Farming Using Satellite Imagery

Research Project ‚Äì Multi-Scale Fusion for Enhanced Crop Mapping

This project focused on improving crop-type mapping and yield estimation for smallholder farmers by integrating multi-scale remote sensing data. We developed an AI-based fusion pipeline that combines low-resolution Sentinel-2 satellite data with high-resolution drone imagery to produce highly accurate spatial and spectral representations of farmland.

The system is designed to function even in low-bandwidth settings, and enables better agricultural planning, monitoring, and policy development by improving the resolution and interpretability of satellite-derived insights.

My Contribution: I led the development of the image fusion pipeline, including pre-processing, alignment, and deep learning-based integration of multi-source imagery to improve crop-type classification and prediction accuracy.

AI for Education in Low-Connectivity Environments

Applied AI Project ‚Äì Inclusive Educational Access through LLMs (Ongoing)

This ongoing project aims to make AI-powered educational support accessible in underserved regions, particularly in communities with limited or no internet connectivity. The system is being built to operate through offline mobile apps, USSD-based queries, and Interactive Voice Response (IVR)‚Äîmaking it usable even on basic phones.

At its core is a fine-tuned LLaMA 2 model, trained on curriculum-aligned content and localized learning materials. The platform is designed to guide students in self-paced learning, track their progress using reinforcement learning, and provide automated evaluations to support learning outcomes. It also enables teachers to deliver personalized learning experiences based on each student's performance and needs.

My Contribution: I'm leading the model fine-tuning with curriculum-aligned content and building the AI-powered interface that enables educational Q&A and progress tracking through offline channels like USSD and IVR.
